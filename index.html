Score

Youngjae graduated from Berklee College of Music with a degree in Film Scoring. She has worked as a film composer, producer, and audio engineer. She was twice nominated for the "Best Score" category at the La Jolla Fashion Filk Festival and LA Web Series Film Festival, and she also won the award for "Best Music Score" at the Wide Screen Film Festival in Miami. Her latest album, Sketchbook, is available now in the iTunes Store and on Spotify.

Yoga

Youngjae is a certified yoga instructor with both a 200-hour Hot Power Fusion and a 200-hour Vinyasa yoga certification. She is an instructor at CorePower Yoga and has also taught local community and corporate classes for companies in the Austin area. She has a deep interest in sound, movement and the intersection between them and seeks to incorporate her musical vision into yoga sequences.
