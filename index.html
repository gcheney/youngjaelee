Score

Youngjae graduated from Berklee College of Music with a degree in Film Scoring. After finishing her degree, she moved to Los Angeles and worked as a film composer, producer, and audio engineer. She was twice nominated for the "Best Score" category at the La Jolla Fashion Filk Festival and LA Web Series Film Festival, and she also won the award for "Best Music Score" at the Wide Screen Film Festival in Miami. She now calls Austin, Texas home and continues to work on new film and music projects.

Yoga

Youngjae is a certified yoga instructor with both a 200-hour Hot Power Fusion and a 200-hour Vinyasa yoga certification. She is an instructor at CorePower Yoga in Austin and has also taught local community classes and corporate classes for companies in the Austin area. She has a deep interest in the intersection of movement and music within yoga, which has lead her to produce a full-length album of music for her yoga class.
