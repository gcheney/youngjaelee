Score

New album In The Moment is now available!

Youngjae graduated from Berklee College of Music with a degree in Film Scoring. She has worked as a film composer and piano instructor. She was twice nominated for the "Best Score" category at the La Jolla International Fashion Film Festival, and she also won the award for "Best Music Score" at the Wide Screen Film Festival in Miami. Her latest album, In The Moment, is available now in the iTunes Store and on Spotify. She has also been teaching piano privately for 10 years. Please contact her if you are interested in a lesson.
