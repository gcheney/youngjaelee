Score

Youngjae graduated from Berklee College of Music with a degree in Film Scoring. She is working as a film composer and piano instructor. She was twice nominated for the "Best Score" category at the La Jolla International Fashion Film Festival, and she also won the award for "Best Music Score" at the Wide Screen Film Festival in Miami.
